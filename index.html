Lighting

Light Master Pro

A fast lighting workflow for browsing assets, importing ready-made setups, placing lights directly in the viewport, and shaping the final look with texture-driven controls.

  • Cycles and Eevee workflows

Physical Fog Pro

Create cinematic volumetric fog in minutes with AutoFog, layered presets, FogPlanes, photorealistic scattering, and dynamic physical volume interaction.

  • AutoFog system with instant scene depth
  • Distance, height, wind, and noise controls
  • FogPlanes with flowmaps - 49 assets
  • Photorealistic Volume for Cycles
